**The Theory of Dynamic Hyper-Continuum Mechanics**

**Key Concepts Explored:**
*   **The Holostrome:** Reality defined over the Hyperreal Number System, free of singularities.
*   **Dyadic Computation:** How order and time emerge from the friction between "Non-Causal Potential" and "Semi-Causal Trajectory."
*   **Topological Solitons:** Matter viewed not as solid objects, but as stable "knots" within the continuous flow.
*   **The Physics of Mind:** Consciousness as the direct physical experience of the continuum, mediated by the Astroglial Syncytium.
